This Cubeibi recipe is the only one you will ever need!

It is very easy to follow and any one can make ot as long as they have a Coca Cola, Captain Morgan and time to drunk text your friends telling them you love them

You will need the following ingredients:

  • Coca Cola
  • Captain Morgan
  • Ice
  • Red plastic cup
  • A spoon

These are the steps to make the perfect Cubeibi:

  1. Fill with Ice until half of the red cup is full.
  2. Pour 2 ounces of Captain Morgan
  3. Pour coca cola until glass is full
  4. Mix with a spoon
  5. Enjoy!
